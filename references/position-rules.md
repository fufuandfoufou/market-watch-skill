# Position Rules

Use these rules for existing positions, especially when the user is trapped and needs practical next steps rather than abstract analysis.

## Four-part structure

For each position, always produce:
1. **观望条件** — when to do nothing
2. **加仓条件** — only after strength confirmation, never just because cost is above price
3. **减仓条件** — where to reduce into strength / failed rebound
4. **止弱条件** — where the trade idea is weakening and defense matters more

## Why this structure works

It prevents two common mistakes:
- averaging down into a falling name with no confirmation
- refusing to reduce because the position is still below cost

## Good defaults

### Add trigger
Usually a reclaim of:
- short-term high (`high5` / `high10`), or
- a short moving average after consolidation

### Reduce trigger
Usually:
- a rebound into resistance,
- or a bounce near a cost-reduction zone where volume fails to confirm

### Fail trigger
Usually:
- break of recent swing low,
- break of `low5` / `low10`,
- or loss of MA10 / MA20 when the setup relied on trend repair

## Communication style

Be direct:
- "先观望" is better than pretending certainty
- "只有站上 X 再考虑小补" is better than saying "can consider adding"
- "跌破 Y 说明短线转弱" is more useful than generic risk warnings

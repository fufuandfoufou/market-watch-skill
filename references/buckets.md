# Candidate Buckets

## A股

### 核心池
More liquid, cleaner trend structure, usually better for steadier next-day observation.

Typical traits:
- larger market cap / stronger liquidity
- cleaner MA structure
- less dependent on one pure theme burst

### 弹性池
Names with stronger turnover, sharper short-term movement, and more upside/downside.

Typical traits:
- high turnover
- strong recent 5-day return
- stronger volume expansion

### 题材池
Names with stronger theme heat, limit-up behavior, or fast emotional rotation.

Typical traits:
- limit-up / near limit-up behavior
- theme-driven bursts
- should be monitored with stricter risk control

## 港股

### 核心大票池
Large caps and heavyweights that anchor watchlists.

### 核心观察池
Not necessarily the biggest names, but tradable and structurally cleaner.

### 弹性机会池
Higher beta names, often with sector momentum.

### 主题/ETF池
Theme proxies, leveraged products, or names whose main value is tactical exposure.

## Why bucket at all?

A single ranking across the whole market is noisy. Buckets make the output more stable and easier to interpret.
